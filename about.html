About

About

We care about matching great, dedicated and committed members of staff.
We only match what we believe is the truly best qualified individuals and we only work with the very best in healthcare providers.
A new way of recruiting for the healthcare sector.

We know that time is important to you so let us do the heavy lifting.  If you are looking to start your career in healthcare then you've found us and that's a great start!


With over 20 combined years of experience in the healthcare sector operating at every single level, it's fair to say there isn't a lot we don't know about.  We are always looking to improve and streamline often outdated practices which give us more time to focus on our clients.  We choose to embrace technology and use it to generate a more streamlined and efficient workflow allowing us to over deliver the best available candidates.

For healthcare professionals we know your time is just as important.  We only work with the best of employers to ensure that when you apply for a role that we're advertising, you can be sure that we have spoken at length with the employers we advertise on behalf of.
What we do?
Well we have listened.  To healthcare professionals and to companies providing healthcare.


We survey the trends in the healthcare industry and when our advanced metrics show us the demand for qualified professionals are about to increase, we start interviewing more prospective candidates.
We enter the funnelling process allowing us to select the creme of the local area, using pre-existing contacts and local knowledge and by keeping our fingers firmly on the pulse in what is our specialist area.
We now have a selection of pre-qualified, enthusiastic and dedicated candidates looking to join a reputable company.  Let us do the hard work so that you can rest easy that you are getting the very best candidates in front of you.

We believe that everything good starts with a single phone call so go and make the best decision, pick up the telephone and give us a call.  We'd only be too pleased to hear from you and to assist you make your next advances in the healthcare industry.  

If you are ready to make a change then we are here to support you and to help you on your fulfilling healthcare journey.
